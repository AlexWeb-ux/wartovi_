About the collection

  • Each NFT combines three elements:

    a poem by a Ukrainian author that directly or metaphorically describes war events in Ukraine;

    a painting by a Ukrainian artist that has semantic references close to the poem;

    sound accompaniment — reading of the poem by Olena Kravets.

  • What do we add to each NFT:

    detailed information about the authors — poet and artist;

    a short comment by the author of the picture about his work;

    a description of the real events against which the poem was created.

Roadmap

Creation of NFT exhibits that will be part of the WARTOVI collection.
Creating a virtual exhibition. Digital innovations allow to fully convey the emotions of NFT authors and bring them to life through reading by Olena Kravets.
Creation of a modern cultural and historical Ukrainian platform. There, representatives of all creative professions will be able to publish their work.

About the project

The project's name reflects the art's main purpose — to guard the inner world of a person, reflect the outer world, and record time.

Our project is designed to draw attention to creative Ukrainians. Among them are many masters and those whose creativity was "awakened" by the war. We want to help them stay inspired in these dark times, just as their creativity helps us keep our humanity and purity of the soul.

The goal of the project is to support creativity in wartime. We are sure that creativity is the metaphysical key to revival, which helps an individual and entire nations survive difficult times.

Today, poets, writers, musicians, and artists have their own front and special mission. The art of wartime is a kind of therapy that helps to remain emotionally alive despite the pain and trials of time, to keep faith in kindness, people, and life.

We believe that the artists and poets of the WARTOVI project protect the beauty of our souls with their works. Art has the healing power of love. WARTOVI allows us to feel it, not lose connection with the heart, and preserve the Human in each of us.

Faq

What is NFT and how can it be used?
NFT is a modern way of investment in the market of artistic works, so-called "non-fungible tokens." By buying NFTs, you are investing in a work of art, becoming the owner of a digital collectible that will grow in value. For example, since 2019, the volume of the NFT market has grown by almost 300% per year, and in 2020 it was already $250 million. In the long term, experts predict further market growth and call investments in NFT super profitable with a high probability percentage.
Where does the money go?
The WARTOVI project was created to support creative people (artists and poets), so the funds paid by the buyer are proportionally distributed between the authors of each NFT, going to the charity fund and the development team.
What percentage goes to the fund?
40% of the profit goes to the fund to support Ukrainian artists.
How are the received funds used?
Royalties are 40% of the value of each NFT, meaning that this is exactly the percentage that the artist and poet receive. The rest of the cost is directed to the advertising company, which aims to support Ukrainian creators in wartime and popularize the WARTOVI project. As well as to finance the activities of the team of creators of the project.
When is the collection coming out?
The publication date of the collection is August 23, 2022. This is deeply symbolic because on this day, it will be exactly six months since the beginning of the full-scale military aggression launched against Ukraine by the russian federation.
